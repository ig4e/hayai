# Product Definition: Hayai

## Initial Concept
Hayai is a free and open-source manga reader for Android, a Mihon-based fork focused on usability and features while staying close to upstream for easier updates.

## Target Audience
- **Power Users:** Advanced users who demand deep customization, extensive source support, and powerful organizational tools for large libraries.
- **Upstream Migrators:** Users transitioning from Mihon or Tachiyomi SY who are looking for enhanced features, better performance, and a more refined user interface without losing core functionality.

## Core Vision
- **UI/UX Excellence:** Delivering a modern, intuitive, and visually cohesive interface that prioritizes accessibility and user comfort during long reading sessions.
- **Performance First:** Ensuring lightning-fast page loading, smooth scrolling in lists and grids, and a highly responsive overall application experience.
- **Feature Richness:** Providing a comprehensive suite of tools, from advanced library management to unique reader enhancements, making it a "best-in-class" fork.

## Strategic Focus
- **UI Migration:** A primary development focus on migrating legacy screens to modern Jetpack Compose standards, ensuring a more maintainable and visually consistent codebase.
- **Upstream Alignment:** Maintaining a close relationship with the Mihon upstream to quickly adopt security fixes, performance improvements, and core feature updates.

## Platform Scope
- **Android Only:** A dedicated focus on the Android ecosystem (Android 6.0 and above), leveraging platform-specific APIs and design patterns to provide a first-class mobile experience.
