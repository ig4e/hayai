# Product Guidelines: Hayai

## Prose Style
- **Friendly & Approachable:** We use a welcoming and easy-to-understand tone. Technical concepts should be explained simply where possible, making the app feel accessible and helpful to all users.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Custom Visuals:** Hayai aims for a completely custom and distinctive visual identity. While we leverage modern Android primitives, the design should feel unique to Hayai, setting it apart from other manga readers through a tailored and cohesive aesthetic.

## UX Principles
- **Efficiency First:** We prioritize reducing taps and streamlining navigation. Every feature should be designed for quick access and minimal friction, especially for power users with large libraries.
- **Accessibility-Centric:** We ensure the app is usable by everyone. This includes proper support for screen readers, high contrast, and scalable typography, making manga reading inclusive.
- **Performance-Driven UX:** We maintain high responsiveness and minimal latency. UI interactions should be instantaneous, and background tasks must not interfere with the user's reading flow.

## Development Goals
- **Innovation Focused:** We prioritize the best possible experience for Hayai users. While we value our upstream roots, we are not afraid to deviate and innovate where it leads to a superior product, focusing on delivering unique and powerful features.
